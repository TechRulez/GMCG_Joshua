About the Campaign

The 'Greater Manchester Colleges for Change' campaign aims to raise awareness and funds to support young people experiencing homelessness. By working together, we can provide them with the support they need to build a brighter future.

Our Partner Charities

Mustard Tree

Mustard Tree combats poverty and prevents homelessness by providing essential services and support to those in need.

Greater Manchester's Mayor's Charity

The Mayor's Charity focuses on initiatives that address homelessness and support vulnerable communities in Greater Manchester.

CentrePoint

CentrePoint offers a range of services to help young people experiencing homelessness, including housing, education, and mental health support.
